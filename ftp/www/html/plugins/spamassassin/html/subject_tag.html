Once SpamAssassin determines that an e-mail message is spam, it wil insert the Subject Tag into the beginning of the e-mail subject line. This allows you to set up custom filters on you personal e-mail client to sort spam-flagged e-mail.

For example, if you received a spam message with the following subject line:

Refinance your mortgage!

and your Subject Tag is set to ****SPAM****, the email subject line will be changed to the following:

****SPAM**** Refinance your mortgage!
