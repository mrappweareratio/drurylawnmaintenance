SpamAssassin has a Blacklist feature that allows you to automatically mark certain e-mail addresses as spam regardless of its spam score. To add an e-mail address to your SpamAssassin Blacklist, type the full e-mail address in to the Blacklist textbox.

NOTE: Multiple e-mail address should be separated on separate lines or with a single blank space. DO NOT use commas or semi-colons to separate your e-mail addresses.

Example Blacklist:
greatoffers@domain1.com
freestuff@domain2.biz
newsletter@domain3.net
bob@domain4.com
