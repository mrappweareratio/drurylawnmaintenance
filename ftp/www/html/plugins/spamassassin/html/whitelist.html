SpamAssassin has a Whitelist feature that allows you to automatically mark certain e-mail addresses as valid e-mail regardless of its spam score. (e.g. valid e-mail newsletters). To add an e-mail address to your SpamAssassin Whitelist, type the full e-mail address in to the Whitelist textbox.

NOTE: Multiple e-mail addresses should be separated on separate lines or with a single blank space. DO NOT use commas or semi-colons to separate your e-mail addresses.

Example Whitelist:
bob@domain1.com
newsletter@domain2.biz
mary@domain3.net
info@domain4.com
